Can I get an invoice right after purchasing?

You can register and use InfiniteScale immediately after purchase, and an electronic invoice of the software will be immediately sent to your email.

Can I upgrade to a premium plan after subscribing?

Step1: Log in InfiniteScale, and click the “Upgrade” button in the top right-hand corner.
Step2: Choose the plan you want to upgrade, and click “Upgrade Now” to complete the payment. The plan will be automatically upgraded.

Can I trust InfiniteScale and order securely?

CleverBridge online order form is 100% secure. All data exchanged during the payment process is SSL-secured.
